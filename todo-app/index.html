Privacy Policy — Simple To-Do

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Simple To-Do ("the app") is developed by mdomorffaruk. This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

Data we collect

The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with us or any third party. All data you enter — task descriptions, details, and due dates — is stored locally on your device only, using local app storage. We have no servers and never receive a copy of this data. The app does not request internet access.

Purchases

This app has no in-app purchases or subscriptions.

Advertising and analytics

This app contains no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

Permissions

The app does not request access to your location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any other sensitive device permissions.

Your data

Uninstalling the app deletes all of its local data, including your full task history. There is nothing on a server to delete, because nothing is ever sent to one.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to mdomorffaruk@gmail.com.
