Privacy Policy — Zakat Calculator

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Zakat Calculator ("the app") is developed by mdomorffaruk. This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

Data we collect

The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with us or any third party. All data you enter — financial figures, gold/silver prices, and calculation history — is stored locally on your device only, using a local database. We have no servers and never receive a copy of this data.

Purchases

The optional "Pro" upgrade is a one-time purchase processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment details. Google's handling of purchase data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

Advertising and analytics

This app contains no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

Permissions

The app does not request access to your location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any other sensitive device permissions.

Financial information

All financial figures you enter (cash, gold, silver, business assets, debts, etc.) are used only to perform the Zakat calculation locally on your device. This information is never transmitted anywhere.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to mdomorffaruk@gmail.com.
